Welcome to FiveM

Download FiveM

FiveM is a modification framework for Grand Theft Auto V, allowing you to play on customized multiplayer servers. Download FiveM to enjoy GTA V with a wide variety of mods, scripts, and custom content created by the community.

Visit the official FiveM website to download the latest version of FiveM and get started.

Join a Server

Discover and join a multitude of servers tailored to different play styles. Whether you're into racing, roleplay, or simply looking for a sandbox environment to have fun with friends, FiveM servers have something for everyone.

Use our server browser to find the perfect community to join and start playing today.
